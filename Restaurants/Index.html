Kristin's Favorite Restaurants in Davis California

This site is all about Kristin's favorite city and some of the best places to eat. At least, in my opinion.

Davis California

Davis, California is small town located about 15 miles west of Sacramento. It is a college town, but also a great place for families. There are tons of city and university sponsored activites throughout the year designed to appeal to the diverse population of the town and bring people together.

In addition to the great activities, shopping and recreation, Davis has a diverse food scene with some of the best foods I've ever eaten.
